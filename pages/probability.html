Random Variable $\X: \Omega \mapsto \mathbb X$ mapped subsets of $\Omega$
Observations: $x_1, \ldots, x_N$ single values of ${\text{X}}$
Observation Space $\mathbb X$ possible observations of ${\text{X}}$
Unknown parameter $\theta ∈ \Theta$ parameter of propability function
Estimator $\T: \mathbb X \mapsto \Theta$ $\T({\text{X}}) = \hat{\theta}$, finds $\hat{\theta}$ from ${\text{X}}$
