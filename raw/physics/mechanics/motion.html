Mechanical Motion

Laws of Motion

Motion Translation { Rotation} (Radius $r$)
Strecke/Winkel $\vec x$ $\vec \varphi = \frac{\vec x}{r}$
Geschwindigkeit $\vec v = \dot{\vec x}$ $\vec \omega = \dot{\vec \varphi} = \frac{\vec v}{r}$
Beschleunigung $\vec a = \dot{\vec v} = \ddot{\vec x}$ $\vec \alpha = \dot{\vec \omega} = \ddot{\vec \varphi} = \frac{\vec a}{r}$
Masse/Trägh. $m$ $\Theta = \int_V \vec r^2_\perp {\,\text{d}}m$
Impuls/Drall $\vec p =m \vec v$ $\vec L = {\mathbf{\Theta}} \vec \omega = \vec r \times \vec p$
Kraft/Moment $\vec F = \dot{\vec p} = m \vec a$ $\vec M = \dot{\vec L} = {\mathbf{\Theta}} \vec \alpha = \vec r \times \vec F$
Energie $E_{{\rm}kin}=\frac12mv^2$ $E_{{\rm}rot}=\frac12 \Theta \omega ^2$
Leistung $P = \vec F {\boldsymbol \cdot}\vec v$ $P = \vec M {\boldsymbol \cdot}\vec \omega$
